Bio

I am a PhD Candidate in Biomedical Engineering at Vanderbilt University. My research employs computational analyses of multimodal neuroimaging data (functional MRI, diffusion MRI, PET, EEG) to better understand epilepsy and develop methods to help guide epilepsy treatment. I am broadly interested in analyzing multimodal neuroimaging data to gain insights into both clinical and basic neuroscience.
